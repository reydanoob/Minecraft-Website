Tutorial 1:
How to start your
first world

Step 1:

Click the "Singleplayer" button on the homepage.

Step 2:

Press "Create New World" and fill in the
options like the seed and difficulty etc. You
don't need to change the seed but you can
find out seeds with better spawn places if you wish to do so.

Step 3:

When you think you are done with your
settings press "Create World" and wait for
the game to load your world. You are all set
for now! Check the next tutorial to see what
you should do when you start your world.
